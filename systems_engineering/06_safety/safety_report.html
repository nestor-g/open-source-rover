Fault Tree Cut Sets

H-01 — Motor Overcurrent

Minimal cut sets (each is sufficient to cause the top event):

  1. Current sensor fails AND Software threshold not reached
  2. RoboClaw ignores setpoint = 0 AND SF-06.4 bug

H-02 — Tip / Rollover

Minimal cut sets (each is sufficient to cause the top event):

  1. IMU data frozen AND Staleness check bypassed
  2. IMU orientation wrong AND Tilt threshold on wrong axis
  3. Rover speed > 0.4 m/s AND Velocity clamp bypassed

H-04 — Runaway After Comm Loss
